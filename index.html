Our Goal

At Depresso Expresso we strive to serve the best coffee and bakery food to make your depression and stress go away for sometime in your life.

Our fall special cinnamon blitz cappuccino.  

The origins

Depresso Expresso came to be when Janet Malaska, our founder, suffered depression during her life. She was able to find joy in picking up a hobby. Making coffee helped her through her rough times and decided she wanted to help other people suffering from depression as well. Her sister decided to come and help by making fresh bakery food to go with the warm coffee.
